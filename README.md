# DYP — Privacy Policy

Public privacy policy for **DYP: drag your plan and actualize it** — a local-only,
plan-vs-actual time reflection app for iOS.

- 한국어: https://jongwyi.github.io/dyp/privacy-ko.html
- English: https://jongwyi.github.io/dyp/privacy-en.html

DYP collects **no personal data whatsoever**. Everything you write or record stays on
your device and is never transmitted to anyone, including the developer. There is no
server, account, analytics, or networking code in the app.

Effective date: 2026-07-06
